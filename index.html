About Blayce

I've been chasing the things under the bed since I was old enough to hold a flashlight. Now I point a camera at them instead. Every upload is one part folklore, one part found-footage, and one part "let's figure this out together, with the lights low and the door locked."

No jump-scare farming, no cheap gore — just slow-build dread, real research, and the occasional candle that flickers when it really shouldn't.
